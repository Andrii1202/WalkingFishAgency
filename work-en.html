New Gold

  • Commercial
  • Branding

New Gold is a premium jewelry brand, which focuses on the simplified geometric shapes in its production.

Communication

To facilitate communication with customers and retailers, we created a universal corporate style with the slogan “New version of you”. It helps to emphasize not only the status of the client, but also his individuality and originality, to look at his values in a different way. These elements are the source of inspiration for the jewelers working on the NEW GOLD collections.

Design

DesignThe NEW GOLD logo consists of small monochromatic rectangles, which you can see also in other design elements. This laconism is complemented by a minimalistic color palette and simple composition that customers associate with elegance, quality and authority.
