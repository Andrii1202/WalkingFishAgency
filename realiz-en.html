CREATING YOUR PROJECT IN 7 STEPS

The animation process of every project is different and unique. That`s why in the end you will get a product that will not only meet your expectations, but even exceed them.


We have prepared a brief description of all the steps of our production process, so each client could familiarize himself with our methodology.

CREATING YOUR PROJECT IN 7 STEPS

Step 1 - Briefing

We analyze your priorities and goals to understand how can we convey your story along with your target audience in the best way. Your deadlines and budget requirements are very important to us that`s why we have an individual approach for every project, which helps us to create a product that not only meets all your criteria, but even exceed them.

Step 2 - Scripting

Script is one of the major elements that decide about the success of project. If you don`t have it, we help to form your own story, which you always will be able to amend and steer. In case if have already a scenario we will be happy to suggest you how it could be improved and epitomized.

Step 3 - Storyboard

On the basis of your brand guidelines presented earlier and our brief we create a number of key frames to show you how your animation/video will look like. This stage includes not only storyboard preparing, but also detailed description that gives you the better understanding of production.

Step 4 - Voice Over

We also assist you in finding an ideal voice matching your brand requirements. Walking Fish helps you to choose the most suitable option and modify it or create a new audio track.

Step 5 - Production

It is the longest part of project taking usually from one to few weeks, depending on your requirements, type of product and length of the voice over. That`s why our collaboration is a key factor here.

Step 6 - Audio Mix

After production process we mix in pre-determined music track and add the relevant sound effects.

Step 7 - Post-production

It`s the final step, on which you will get completed file. The last thing for you is just to enjoy the product and don`t forget to use our discount for next orders.
