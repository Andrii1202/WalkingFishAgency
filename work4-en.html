Advertising from Walking Fish for Balsamico Salsa. It's a natural balsamic sauce, produced according to the original recipe with fresh syrup including natural ingredients only.

The sauce has a sweet and sour taste with pleasant aroma of grapes, cherries or currants. Balsamic sauce is the best dressing for salads, as well as for meat, fish and cheese. Also, it is an excellent basis for the eggs and seafood cooking. In addition, balsamic sauce gives a unique taste to desserts and fruit dishes, especially with strawberries, peaches and other.
